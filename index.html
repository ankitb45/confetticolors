About the Artist

Colour as a
language

Hello! I'm the artist behind Confetti Colors — a self-taught painter who found her voice through acrylic on canvas. No formal training, just years of curiosity, play, and a deep love for the way paint moves.

My work explores texture, earthy palettes, and the intuitive mark-making that happens when you stop overthinking and just let the brush lead. Every painting begins as an experiment and ends as a feeling.

Based in Toronto, creating for walls, hearts, and anyone who believes a home deserves art that breathes.

Behind the Canvas

How a painting
comes to life

01

Intuition First

Every painting starts with a feeling, not a plan. I layer colour instinctively, letting the canvas tell me where to go before my mind can interrupt.

02

Texture & Build

Acrylics let me build up richness — thick impasto marks, translucent glazes, scraped edges. The texture is as much a part of the work as the colour.

03

Finding the Finish

I know a painting is done when it surprises me. When there's nothing left to add and nothing I want to take away. That moment is the whole reason I paint.
